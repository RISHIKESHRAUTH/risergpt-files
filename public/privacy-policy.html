Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 17, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to RiserGPT. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data when you use our AI platform and authentication services.

2. Data We Collect

To provide a seamless AI experience, we collect the following information:

  • Account Data: Email address, name, and profile information provided via Google OAuth or email registration.
  • Interaction Data: The prompts and inputs you provide to the AI for processing and response generation.
  • Authentication Artifacts: OTP codes, session tokens, and security alert logs.
  • Device Data: IP addresses, browser types, and operating system information for security and optimization.

3. How We Use Data

Your data is used strictly for the following purposes:

  • Providing AI-driven chat and analysis services.
  • Ensuring account security through multi-factor authentication (OTP).
  • Improving the quality and accuracy of our AI models.
  • Sending critical security alerts and password reset notifications.

4. Data Security

RiserGPT employs enterprise-grade encryption and security protocols (AES-256) to protect your data. All interactions are transmitted over secure TLS channels. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

5. Cookies & Tracking

We use essential cookies to manage your login sessions and security preferences. We do not use intrusive tracking or third-party marketing cookies that compromise your privacy.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, export, or delete your RiserGPT account data at any time. Requests for data deletion can be initiated through your account settings or by contacting our security team.
